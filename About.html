About Rizwan Ullah

Rizwan Ullah is the founder and managing director of Ullah and Associates Chartered Accountants. With over 15 years of experience in the financial industry, Rizwan has established himself as a highly skilled and reputable Chartered Accountant. He has worked with various public and private sector organizations, providing exceptional accounting services and business consulting.

Rizwan holds a BA (Hons) in Business from the Open University and a Diploma in Computing from Leeds Met. He is also a Chartered Public Financial Accountant (CPFA), a certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA), and holds a Comptia Security+ certification.

His expertise includes tax planning, financial management, business strategy, and risk management. Rizwan's advanced data analytics skills and programming experience enable him to leverage cutting-edge technologies to deliver efficient and innovative solutions to his clients.

As a dedicated professional, Rizwan is committed to helping businesses grow through strategic decision-making and effective risk management. His passion for excellence and strong leadership skills ensure that Ullah and Associates Chartered Accountants remains at the forefront of the industry, delivering top-notch services to its clients.
